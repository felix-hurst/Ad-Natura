=== (E.3) Constraints

Our game will consist of a maximum of 4 unique levels of different challenges and environmental backdrop. The player will have in their inventory at any one time a maximum of 3 tools in which to manipulate the environment. The game shall take up no more than 2gb of the playerâ€™s system storage. Maximum vertices that should be considered when deforming a game object or terrain is 4. The game shall introduce no more core game mechanics other than destructible environment and slime mold pathway. We are aiming for a minimum of 30 fps for our game at the most computationally intensive areas.
